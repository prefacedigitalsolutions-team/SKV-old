About the company

SKV Glass & Interiors

The history of the company goes back into the year 2021, when SKTL came into being and it was primarily engaged in Furniture & Interior projects. Later, we expanded our horizons and got into the manufacturing of Decorative glass in the year 2024 & a new company was formed with the name of SKV Glass & interiors. We have a manufacturing unit, near IMT Manesar, Gurugram, equipped with all advanced machineries & equipments to produce high quality decorative glass. SKV glass is run by a team of professionals having industry experience of more than 15 years & deep insight about the products. Our skilled & dedicated workforce of about 30 persons brings our imagination into a real masterpiece. We have in-house machineries comprising of Grinding machine, lamination, Paint Booth, Glass washing, Corner edge machine. Paint lab, Cutting, Milling, Punching, Drilling to manufacture, monitor and produce each & every product in a quality controlled environment.

Our Product range encompasses Fabric Laminated Glass, Tinted Glass, Wire Mesh Glass, Clear Glass, Marble Glass, Reeded Glass & Laquered Glass. Today SKV Glass is the first choice of every interior designer and architect alike for their aesthetic value, versatility and longer life. These products are being used in Workspaces, Glass Partitions, Cubicles, Screens, Furniture, Table Tops, Kitchen & Wardrobe Shutters and Bathroom Shower enclosures. In a short span of time , SKV Glass has carved a niche for itself in the premium decorative glass segment by delivering no-compromise quality products, fulfilling commitments adhering to the timelines & offering value for money to its customer, Pan India.
